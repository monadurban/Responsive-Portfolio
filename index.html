About Me


Hello, and welcome to my first design portfolio webpage. I am learning how to make a simple but effective site with my introductory web design skills for others to peruse.


My background is in healthcare where my skills were honed to listen to and understand patient needs and develop solutions to their problems or issues.


I have a B.S. in Biology and a Doctor of Optometry with over a decade of full time clinical experience. In addition, I have presented posters at national conferences, worked as a subcontractor writing medical articles, and given healthcare presentations to my community at large.


Feel free to explore this site and I look forward to getting to know you through my contact page!
